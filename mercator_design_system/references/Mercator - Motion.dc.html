Mercator
Motion · v1.0
Motion confirms. It never performs.
An instrument doesn't bounce. Chrome moves in ≤200ms and settles without overshoot; only computation — a run, a figure finding its value, a chart drawing — may take longer, because there the motion is the information. Every demo below is live — press Replay.
micro · 120ms base · 160ms enter · 200ms process · data-led
I · Principles
01
Restraint
Anything that isn't data resolves in ≤200ms. If you notice the animation, it's too much.
02
One mover
The eye follows a single change. Stagger or sequence — never animate six things at once.
03
Settle, don't bounce
Decelerate to rest. No overshoot, no spring — easing that calls no attention to itself.
04
Data earns duration
Only computation may exceed 200ms — the run, a figure settling, a chart drawing in.
